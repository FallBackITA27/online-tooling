symmetry is a clan Ideated by Leila and founded together with Arthur'Oww, as a way to somehow mimick TrackMania clans inside of MKW.

The clan was founded on the 26th of March 2024, and the name was proposed by Danny Boy, not even coming close in a poll with other proposed names.

  1. Symmetry: 25 Votes
  2. Organized: 19 Votes
  3. Mafia: 18 Votes
  4. Quantum Network: 15 Votes
  5. Quantum Organization: 13 Votes

We realized at a second moment that the clan tag SY was already in use by a GSC clan, we decided to lowercase it as there wouldn't be much overlap anyways.

We currently host a total of 9 members, 5 of which are European and the remaining 4 are North American.
